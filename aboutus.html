What Interact Means to Us

Interact Club unites student leaders by introducing them to a lifetime of service. Interactors find purpose in effecting change in their communities and connecting through goodwill. - District 5300

Interact is a self-governed organization for youths between ages 12 and 18 throughout the world, who try to help the community while learning the qualities of a leader. We are partially sponsored by Rotary Club, whose members offer guidance and connections to find city-wide services. Our goals are to have fun while making a difference, one small step at a time. - MKHS

Interact Clubs are found in other schools around the globe and we work together to make our communities and world a better place. Working towards helping the unfortunate or underprivileged is our main goal. - SGHS

The purpose of Interact is to provide opportunity for young people to work together in a world fellowship dedicated to service and international understanding. - GHS

We are a service based organization across the country. We follow our motto "Service Above Self" by helping our community out. We will not only help with our own school, but also help the schools around us and third-world countries. - TCHS

Interact is an international service club that is affiliated with the Rotary Association. Our motto is "Service Above Self." Our purpose is to organize volunteering opportunities, document hours, and promote community & international action. - AHS
